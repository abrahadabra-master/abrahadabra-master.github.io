Calendar of Events

While things can change unexpectedly and quickly, we endeavor to keep our calendar accurate to at least a week ahead of time. Check back here for the most up-to-date information on events, and contact us via email with any questions.

Unless stated otherwise, all events are open to the public. For more information, please feel free to contact us.

We also host a public Google Calendar which you can access here

Monthly Social Hour

Wednesday, November 27 6:00-8:00 PM

Come join us on the last Wednesday of the month for a public social event in a relaxed, casual atmosphere. Weather permitting will be outside on the deck. Look for the red Book of the Law to find us. No RSVP is necessary, but helpful if you haven't attended before so we can be on the lookout for you. No agenda or topic of conversation, however topics generally range from O.T.O. and E.G.C. discussions too Tarot (bring your favorite deck!), Qabbalah, Hermeticism, Alchemy, or generally just what is going on. This is a great opportunity to meet fellow Thelemites and get to know each other. We'll be there from 6pm until 8pm. Bring your questions and get to know your local Thelemites and members of Abrahadabra Oasis.

Bring your questions and get to know your local Thelemites!

Location: Novare Res Bier Cafe, 4 Canal Plaza #1, Portland, ME 04101

Second (IIº) Initiations

Saturday, April 20

Abrahadabra Oasis will be performing Second Degree (IIº) initiations on April 20.
