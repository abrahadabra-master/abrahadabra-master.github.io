Calendar of Events

COVID-19

All in-person events depend on the covid policy set by U.S. Grand Lodge, which you can find at https://admin.oto-usa.org/covid19. While things can change unexpectedly and quickly, we endeavor to keep our calendar accurate to at least a week ahead of time. Check back here for the most up-to-date information on events, and contact us via email with any questions. Where events are held at a public location, the COVID-19 policy of the establishment takes precedent.


Unless stated otherwise, all events are open to the public. For more information, please feel free to contact us.

January

January 21

First Degree Initiations

Members of First Degree and above only. If you're at least a First degree initiate of O.T.O. in good standing, please contact Abrahadabra's Master for information on attending.

January 31
Tuesday
6:00-8:00 PM

Monthly Happy Hour

Come join us on the last Tuesday of the month for a public social event in a relaxed, casual atmosphere. Will try and get a table, but look for the red Book of the Law to find us. No RSVP is necessary, but helpful if you haven't attended before so we can be on the lookout for you.
We'll be there by 6pm until at least 8pm. Feel free to stop by any time during. Bring your questions and get to know your local Thelemites. Note that everyone is responsible for the costs of their own food and drink.

Location: LFK, 188 State St, Portland, ME 04102, USA
