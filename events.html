Calendar of Events

While things can change unexpectedly and quickly, we endeavor to keep our calendar accurate to at least a week ahead of time. Check back here for the most up-to-date information on events, and contact us via email with any questions.

Unless stated otherwise, all events are open to the public. For more information, please feel free to contact us.

We also host a public Google Calendar which you can access here

Monthly Social Hour

Wednesday, January 31 7:00-8:00 PM

Come join us on the last Wednesday of the month for a public social event in a relaxed, casual atmosphere. Will try and get a table, but look for the red Book of the Law to find us. No RSVP is necessary, but helpful if you haven't attended before so we can be on the lookout for you.

Bring your questions and get to know your local Thelemites!

Location: Novel, 643 Congress St, Portland, ME 04101, USA
