Ordo Templi Orientis

Do what thou wilt shall be the whole of the Law.

Ordo Templi Orientis is a fraternal organization, admitting both men and women, dedicated to promoting the principles of the philosophical and spiritual philosophies of Thelema as expounded by its principal prophet and scholar, Aleister Crowley. Its structure is based on a successive series of initiatory rituals that serve to instruct and guide the individual, conferring upon him or her a specific degree or grade in the style of traditional Freemasonry, from which OTO originates. For more on the initiatory structure of OTO, please see the USGL Initiation and Membership pages.

As a principal function of its mission, Abrahadabra Oasis provides individuals the opportunity for initiation through the Third Degree in OTO. Membership is open to all individuals that are free, of full age, and of good report, without consideration of race, ethnicity, sex, gender identity, or sexual orientation. For more information on membership in OTO, and specifically your involvement through Abrahadabra Oasis, please feel free to contact us.
