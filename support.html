Support Abrahadabra Oasis

Abrahadabra Oasis, O.T.O., depends on the support of its members and the community to maintain its temple space in Portland, Maine. This space provides the ability for us to consistently celebrate the Gnostic Mass, offer initiations, teach classes related to Thelema and magick, and serve as a meeting place for the Thelemic community in and around the greater Portland area.

We need your support. Without the financial assistance of our membership and the greater Thelemic community, we could not offer the same level and quality of service to our community. Please give what you can, using the donation buttons on this page for either one-time donations or monthly dues commitments. Anything helps!

As a chartered body of Ordo Templi Orientis, donations to Abrahadabra Oasis are tax deductible under the provisions of a 501(c)3 institution.
