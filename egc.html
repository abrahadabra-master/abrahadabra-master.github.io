Ecclesia Gnostica Catholica

Greeting of Earth and Heaven!

The letters EGC stand for Ecclesia Gnostica Catholica, or the Gnostic Catholic Church. It represents the religious arm of OTO, celebrating the Gnostic Mass as its central ritual as well as performing other ecclesiastical duties such as baptisms, confirmations, and ordinations.

Abrahadabra Oasis counts among its membership several ordained clergy of EGC, and offers all of the services mentioned above. (Ordination, however, is contingent upon attaining certain degrees within OTO based upon the level of ordination.) We also celebrate the Gnostic Mass at least monthly, and these celebrations are generally open to the public. See our events calendar for upcoming dates.

More information can be found at the EGC page of the United States Grand Lodge web site.

The LORD bring you to the accomplishment of your true Wills, the Great Work,
the Summum Bonum, True Wisdom and Perfect Happiness.
