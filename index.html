Abrahadabra Oasis is the local body of Ordo Templi Orientis in the Valley of Portland, Maine. Our mission is to provide the communities of northern New England with a place to gather, grow, and live according to the tenets of Thelema as expounded by its principal prophet, Aleister Crowley, and its principal text, The Book of the Law.
